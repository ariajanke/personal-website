Mag Feeder Web App

Preview mag feed plans or explore possible mags for PSO in the fraction of the time it takes to feed one in game. So far there are accuracy problems. If you'd like to improve this app, feel free to send suggestions, corrections, and feature requests. I am aware of the presence of Magatama. The motivation of developing this app is more personal. My hope is that this project may help me out of my depressive state.

Core Features

  • Character Selection
  • Item Feeding
  • (planned) Accurate to in game mag feeding

Additional Features

  • Feed History
  • Save/Load mags
  • Feed Item preview
  • (planned) Mag Cells
